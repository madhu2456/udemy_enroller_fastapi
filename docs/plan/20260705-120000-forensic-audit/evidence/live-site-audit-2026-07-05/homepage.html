FAQ

How does it work?

Monitors coupon sites 24/7 and automatically applies valid 100% off coupons to your account.

Is my account safe?

Yes. Passwords are never stored. Cookies are encrypted. Uses Udemy's own endpoints.

Email or Cookie login?

Cookie Login is more reliable. Email may trigger CSRF errors on servers.

Really free?

100% free and open source on GitHub.

Can I filter which courses get enrolled?

Yes. Set minimum rating, preferred language, and exclude specific instructors.

How do I get started?

Log into Udemy, extract your cookies using the Chrome extension, and paste them into the Cookie Login form.

Will Udemy ban my account?

The tool uses respectful rate limiting and the same endpoints as the Udemy website. However, automated access may violate Udemy's Terms of Service. Users are responsible for compliance.

Does it still work in 2026?

Yes. The tool is actively maintained with regular updates to coupon scrapers and enrollment logic. Check the GitHub repository for the latest version.
