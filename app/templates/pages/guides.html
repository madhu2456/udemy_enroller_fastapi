Getting Started

New to the Udemy Course Enroller? This guide walks you through connecting your account and running your first automated enrollment in under 5 minutes.

- Visit the homepage and click Start Automating.
- Log into udemy.com in your browser.
- Extract cookies (F12 → Application → Cookies).
- Paste access_token, client_id, and csrftoken.
- Click Connect via Cookies to begin automation.

Cookie Login Deep Dive

Cookie-based authentication is the most reliable way to use the enroller. Learn exactly where to find each cookie and how to keep your session alive.

access_token client_id csrftoken

Pro tip: If your session expires, simply re-extract fresh cookies from your browser. The enroller will automatically update your stored session tokens upon connecting.

Advanced Filtering

Stop enrolling courses you'll never watch. Use smart filters to build a library that actually matches your learning goals.

- Categories: Development, Business, Design, Marketing, etc.
- Languages: English, Spanish, Hindi, and more.
- Min Rating: Filter out courses below your quality threshold (0.0 – 5.0).
- Instructor Blocklist: Exclude specific instructors by username.
