Getting Started

New to the Udemy Course Enroller? This guide walks you through creating an account, connecting your Udemy session, and running your first automated enrollment in under 5 minutes.

  • 1. Visit the homepage and click Start Automating
  • 2. Log into udemy.com in your browser
  • 3. Extract cookies (F12 → Application → Cookies)
  • 4. Paste access_token, client_id, and csrftoken
  • 5. Click Connect via Cookies and head to Dashboard

Advanced Filtering

Stop enrolling courses you'll never watch. Use smart filters to build a library that actually matches your learning goals.

  • •Categories: Development, Business, Design, Marketing, IT & Software, etc.
  • •Languages: English, Spanish, Hindi, and more
  • •Min Rating: Filter out courses below your quality threshold (0.0 – 5.0)
  • •Instructor Blocklist: Exclude specific instructors by username
  • •Title Keywords: Skip courses containing unwanted terms

All filters are saved per-user and persist across sessions. Adjust them anytime in the Settings page.

Self-Hosting with Docker

Want full control? Deploy the Udemy Course Enroller on your own server or VPS using the included Docker setup.

git clone <repo-url> cd udemy-enroller docker-compose up -d

The stack includes FastAPI, SQLite, and Alembic migrations. Environment variables are loaded from .env.
