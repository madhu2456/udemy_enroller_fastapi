About Udemy Enroller

Udemy Enroller is a production FastAPI automation platform built by Madhu Dadi. It coordinates asynchronous enrollment workflows using FastAPI, Playwright, Celery, Redis, PostgreSQL, Docker, and real-time telemetry.

The platform is designed to demonstrate production-grade workflow orchestration, bounded concurrency, secure session-state handling, background processing, and operational logging.

Udemy Enroller is an independent project and is not affiliated with Udemy, Inc.
